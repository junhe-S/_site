Other Data Providers

Jun He May 31, 2026

WRDS is not the only source, and for many questions free or low-cost providers are enough. The skill is bringing heterogeneous sources into the same tidy workflow.

Free and low-cost sources

Public APIs supply daily prices and dividends, exchange-rate and interest-rate series, and a wide range of macroeconomic indicators from central banks and statistical agencies. For prototyping or teaching, these often substitute for proprietary feeds.

Trade-offs

Free data comes with caveats: shorter or less reliable history, survivorship issues (delisted firms quietly disappear), and looser quality control. Knowing a source's coverage and biases is part of using it responsibly — a backtest on survivor-only data flatters every strategy.

One workflow

Whatever the source, the goal is the same: pull it with a script, reshape it into a tidy table with consistent keys, and store it alongside everything else. Once standardized, an alternative data source slots into the analysis exactly like CRSP or Compustat would.
