1.2 标准布朗运动的存在性 / Existence of Standard Brownian Motion

1.2.1 简化证明 / Simplify The Proof

我们要证明布朗运动存在。由命题 1.1，只需证明标准布朗运动存在；而要证其存在，只需构造一个标准布朗运动。可作如下简化：把 \(\{B_t:t\in[0,+\infty)\}\) 归一化为 \(\{B_t:t\in[0,1]\}\)——一旦能构造 \(\{B_t:t\in[0,1]\}\)，就能取可数无穷多份 \(\{B_t:t\in[0,1]\}\)、把每份的终点与下一份的起点相连，得到 \(\{B_t:t\in[0,+\infty)\}\)。连续时间难处理，故把 $[0,1]$ 分成无穷多个可数离散点，之后取极限来逼近连续性。

1.2.2 构造路线图 / Road Map of The Construction

分三步证明布朗运动存在：第 1 步：对 \(t\in\mathcal D\) 定义 \(B_t\)，并证明 \(\{B_t:t\in\mathcal D\}\) 满足定义 1.2 的条件 (1)-(3)。第 2 步：证明以概率 1，函数 \(t\to B_t\)（\(t\in\mathcal D\)）一致连续。第 3 步：在第 2 步基础上定义 \(B_s=\lim_{t\to s}B_t\)（\(t\in\mathcal D\)，\(s\in[0,1]\)）(1.3)，并证明 \(\{B_s:s\in[0,1]\}\) 是布朗运动。

1.2.3 第 1 步 / Step 1

定义二进有理数集 \(D_n=\{\frac{j}{2^n}:j=0,1,\dots,2^n\}\)、\(\mathcal D=\bigcup_{n=1}^\infty D_n\)。设概率空间 \((\Omega,\mathcal F,\mathbb P)\) 足够大、可容纳一可数集的独立标准正态变量 \(\mathcal Z=\{N_q:q\in\mathcal D\}\)（\(N_q\sim\mathcal N(0,1)\)，由 \(\mathcal D\) 中元素索引）。

1.2 Existence of Standard Brownian Motion

1.2.1 Simplify The Proof

We want to show the existence of Brownian motion. By Proposition 1.1, we only need to show the existence of a standard Brownian motion; and to show its existence, it suffices to construct one. We can simplify: normalize \(\{B_t:t\in[0,+\infty)\}\) to \(\{B_t:t\in[0,1]\}\) — once we can construct \(\{B_t:t\in[0,1]\}\), we can take a countably infinite collection of copies of \(\{B_t:t\in[0,1]\}\) and connect the end of each with the start of the next to obtain \(\{B_t:t\in[0,+\infty)\}\). Continuous time is hard, so we split $[0,1]$ into countably infinitely many discrete points and take limits later to approximate continuity.

1.2.2 Road Map of The Construction

We prove existence in three steps: Step 1: define \(B_t\) for \(t\in\mathcal D\) and show \(\{B_t:t\in\mathcal D\}\) satisfies conditions (1)-(3) of Definition 1.2. Step 2: show that with probability one the function \(t\to B_t\) for \(t\in\mathcal D\) is uniformly continuous. Step 3: on the basis of Step 2, define \(B_s=\lim_{t\to s}B_t\) (\(t\in\mathcal D\), \(s\in[0,1]\)) (1.3), and show \(\{B_s:s\in[0,1]\}\) is a Brownian motion.

1.2.3 Step 1

Define the dyadic rationals \(D_n=\{\frac{j}{2^n}:j=0,1,\dots,2^n\}\) and \(\mathcal D=\bigcup_{n=1}^\infty D_n\). Suppose the probability space \((\Omega,\mathcal F,\mathbb P)\) is large enough to contain a countable set of independent standard normal variables \(\mathcal Z=\{N_q:q\in\mathcal D\}\) (\(N_q\sim\mathcal N(0,1)\), indexed by elements in \(\mathcal D\)).

1.3 Hölder 连续 / Hölder Continuity

Important

定义 1.5、1.6 与定理 1.2 / Definitions 1.5, 1.6 and Theorem 1.2 定义 1.5（Hölder 连续）：函数 \(f:[0,1]\to\mathbb R^d\) 是阶 \(\alpha\) 的 Hölder 连续，若 \(\exists C<\infty\) 使得 \(\forall s\le t\)，\(|f(t)-f(s)|\le C(t-s)^\alpha\)。注 1.6：\(\alpha>\beta>0\) 时，阶 \(\alpha\) 的 Hölder 连续蕴含阶 \(\beta\) 的 Hölder 连续。注 1.7：\(\alpha>1\) 时定义 1.5 变平凡（要求 \(f\) 为水平直线）：\(|\frac{f(t)-f(s)}{t-s}|\le C(t-s)^{\alpha-1}\)，\(t-s\to0\Rightarrow|\frac{f(t)-f(s)}{t-s}|\to0\)；\(\alpha=1\) 时即 Lipschitz 连续。定义 1.6（弱 Hölder 连续）：\(f:[0,1]\to\mathbb R^d\) 是阶 \(\alpha\) 的弱 Hölder 连续，若对 \(\forall\beta<\alpha\) 它都是阶 \(\beta\) 的 Hölder 连续。定理 1.2：以概率 1，布朗运动 \(\{B_t:t\in[0,1]\}\) 是阶 \(\frac12\) 的弱 Hölder 连续，但不是阶 \(\frac12\) 的 Hölder 连续。Definition 1.5 (Hölder Continuity): a function \(f:[0,1]\to\mathbb R^d\) is Hölder continuous of order \(\alpha\) if \(\exists C<\infty\) such that \(\forall s\le t\), \(|f(t)-f(s)|\le C(t-s)^\alpha\).
