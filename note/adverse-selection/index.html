12. Adverse Selection

Note

Overview This chapter opens the "Adverse Selection" group of Part III, studying two markets with asymmetric information but no communication mechanism yet (signaling, screening, etc. come in later chapters). §12.1 the MWG labor market: nature draws each worker's productivity (type) \(\theta_i\), workers know their own type but firms do not, and firms can only post a uniform wage \(w\); using the supply set \(\Theta(w)\), demand \(D(w)\), and expected productivity \(\mathbb{E}[\theta\mid w]\) we characterize the competitive equilibrium \(\mathbb{E}[\theta\mid w^\star]=w^\star\), then work two examples (constant reservation wage; reservation wage increasing in productivity), the latter exhibiting adverse selection (high-productivity workers have high reservation wages and are less willing to work), possibly with multiple equilibria, and under incomplete information only the lowest-productivity worker or a fraction can be employed. §12.2 the JR insurance market: consumers privately know their own accident probability \(\pi\) and are risk-averse; we derive the cutoff \(\pi\ge h(p)\) for buying insurance and the buyers' expected loss \(g(p)\), with equilibrium \(p^\star=g(p^\star)\); and we distinguish adverse selection from advantageous selection. All figures (Figures 2–7) are paraphrased in prose.

本部分讨论两个存在非对称信息的博弈：一方了解自身类型，而另一方对此一无所知，并且目前不存在任何可能的沟通机制（如后续要讨论的信号或筛选）。我们先刻画这种条件下的市场结果，随后各章再引入沟通机制来修正该结果。

12.1 MWG 劳动力市场问题

This part discusses two games with asymmetric information: one side knows its own type while the other side knows nothing about it, and for now there is no possible communication mechanism (such as the signaling or screening discussed later). We first characterize the market outcome under such conditions, then later chapters add communication mechanisms to modify the outcome.

12.1.2 竞争均衡 / Competitive equilibrium

在竞争均衡里，工人与企业都是价格接受者，均衡工资 \(w^\star\) 使工人的供给等于企业的需求。

12.1.2 Competitive equilibrium

In a competitive equilibrium both workers and firms are price takers, and the equilibrium wage \(w^\star\) makes the supply of workers equal the demand from firms.

Important

供给、需求与均衡 / Supply, demand, equilibrium 工人供给：在给定工资 \(w\) 下愿意工作的工人（按类型）集合记为 \(\Theta(w)\)，见下式。企业需求：给定工资 \(w\) 下企业需求的工人数 \(D(w)\)，依 \(\mathbb{E}[\theta\mid w]\) 与 \(w\) 的大小分三种情形（见下）；其中 \(\mathbb{E}[\theta\mid w]\) 是在工资 \(w\) 下愿意工作的工人的平均（期望）生产率。竞争均衡 \(w^\star\)：供给等于需求要求 \(\mathbb{E}[\theta\mid w^\star]=w^\star\)。Supply of workers: the set of workers (by type) willing to work at wage \(w\) is \(\Theta(w)\), see below. 12.1.4 例 2：保留工资随生产率递增 / Example 2: reservation wage increasing in productivity

假设保留工资 \(r(\theta)\) 随类型 \(\theta\)（生产率）递增，于是产生逆向选择：

  • 生产率更高的工人保留工资也更高，因而更不愿供给劳动；
  • 生产率更低的工人保留工资更低，因而更愿供给劳动。

再假设保留工资不超过生产率，即对任何类型 \(r(\theta)\le\theta\)。

12.1.4 Example 2: reservation wage increasing in productivity

Assume the reservation wage \(r(\theta)\) is increasing in type \(\theta\) (productivity), which produces adverse selection:

  • higher-productivity workers also have higher reservation wages, making them less willing to supply labor;
  • lower-productivity workers have lower reservation wages, making them more willing to supply labor.

Also assume the reservation wage is no greater than productivity for any type, i.e. \(r(\theta)\le\theta\).

If firms could act under complete information, they could pay previously employed workers a wage equal to their individual productivity and thereby employ the whole market. We now formalize the intuition that "with multiple equilibria the SPNE always selects the highest one." We are in the following game:

  1. Firms simultaneously announce wage offers;
  2. Workers then decide whether and for which firm to work; facing several firms offering the same wage, a worker randomizes among them.

Denote the highest equilibrium wage by \(\bar w^\star=\max_{w^\star} CE\).

12.2 JR Insurance Market Problem

Important

设定 / Set-up（基于 JR 2011, Advanced Microeconomic Theory, 3rd Ed.） 消费者风险（类型）：\(\pi\in[\underline\pi,\bar\pi]=\Pi\subseteq[0,1]\)，其累积分布函数为 \(F(\pi)\)，表示消费者发生损失（出险）的概率。若发生损失，损失额为 \(L\)；若不发生，损失为零。工人（消费者）知道自身的出险概率 \(\pi_i\)，企业不知道个体概率，只知道类型分布 \(F\)。消费者风险厌恶，具有严格凹的 VNM 效用 \(U(\cdot)\)，初始财富为 \(y\)。企业风险中性；暂设只有一份合约——全额保险。若企业能以高于精算公平价 \(\pi L\) 的价格出售，则获利。Consumer's risk (type): \(\pi\in[\underline\pi,\bar\pi]=\Pi\subseteq[0,1]\) with c.d.f. \(F(\pi)\), the probability that a consumer suffers a loss (an accident). If the loss happens it equals \(L\); if not, the loss is zero. Workers (consumers) know their own accident probability \(\pi_i\); firms do not know individual probabilities, only the type distribution \(F\). Consumers are risk-averse with strictly concave VNM utility \(U(\cdot)\) and initial wealth \(y\). Firms are risk-neutral; for the moment assume a single contract — full insurance. If a firm can sell at a price above the actuarially fair price \(\pi L\), it makes money.

A consumer's utility without insurance is:

因此，消费者愿意购买保险当且仅当

Hence a consumer will buy insurance if and only if

$$ U(y-p)\ge \pi\cdot U(y-L)+(1-\pi)\,U(y) $$

整理得临界条件（\(h(p)\) 关于 \(p\) 递增，因为效用随财富递增）：

which rearranges to the cutoff condition (\(h(p)\) is increasing in \(p\) because utility increases in money):

$$ \pi\ge \frac{U(y)-U(y-p)}{U(y)-U(y-L)}\equiv h(p) $$

Important

买方期望损失与均衡 / Buyers' expected loss and equilibrium 由临界条件可直接得出存在逆向选择：愿意购买的恰是出险概率高的人。广义地说，逆向选择指未获信息的一方得到的，正是已获信息的一方最不想要的对手；有利选择则相反——最谨慎的人最想买保险，最不愿出险。关键函数为下式 \(g(p)\)，即购买者的期望损失；它类比于劳动力市场里的 \(g(w)\)，并因逆向选择而递增。竞争均衡 \(p^\star\) 满足 \(p^\star=g(p^\star)\)（见其后公式）。The cutoff immediately implies adverse selection: those willing to buy are exactly the high-probability types. Broadly, adverse selection is when the uninformed side gets the counterparties the informed side least wants; advantageous selection is the opposite — the most cautious people most want insurance and least want accidents. The key function is \(g(p)\) below, the expected loss from buyers; it is analogous to \(g(w)\) in the labor market and is increasing due to adverse selection. The competitive equilibrium \(p^\star\) satisfies \(p^\star=g(p^\star)\) (formula after).

$$ g(p)\equiv \mathbb{E}\!\left[\pi\mid \pi\ge h(p)\right]\times L $$

$$ p^\star=g(p^\star)=\mathbb{E}\!\left[\pi\mid \pi\ge h(p^\star)\right]\cdot L $$

Tip

类比劳动力市场的结论 / Results analogous to the labor market (a) 若以价格 \(\underline\pi L\) 出售，则人人都会购买。(b) 低风险类型也会购买，因为有一标准结论：给定可买精算公平的保单，主体会选择完全投保。(c) 高风险类型会购买，因为 \(p=\underline\pi L<\bar\pi L\)。综合言之，竞争均衡可达 \(p^\star=\mathbb{E}[\pi]\,L\) 且所有类型都购买。(a) If insurance is offered at price \(\underline\pi L\) then everyone buys it. (b) Low-risk types also buy, by the standard result that given the option to buy an actuarially fair policy, agents fully insure. (c) High-risk types buy because \(p=\underline\pi L<\bar\pi L\). In sum, the competitive equilibrium can be \(p^\star=\mathbb{E}[\pi]\,L\) with all types purchasing.

[!tip] 注 12.3 逆向选择与有利选择 / Remark 12.3 Adverse vs Advantageous Selection 逆向选择：最愿意为保险付费的人，往往也是风险最高的人。有利选择：当（边际）购买者的边际成本高于全体购买人群的平均成本时出现，因为低风险消费者属于"边际内"（infra-marginal）。在有利选择市场，你有时会得到相对于有效结果过多的产出；这与逆向选择相反——后者相对于有效结果产出过少。这两类模型的另一个有趣特征是：在逆向选择模型中，需求曲线与边际成本曲线之间的距离就是风险溢价；而在有利选择模型中这不成立，需求曲线与边际成本曲线之间的距离是扣除任何固定成本后的风险溢价。Adverse selection: those willing to pay the most for insurance also have the highest risks. Advantageous selection occurs when the marginal cost of the (marginal) buyer is higher than the average cost of the buying population, because low-risk consumers are infra-marginal. In advantageous markets you can sometimes have too much output relative to the efficient outcome; this contrasts with adverse selection where you can have too little output relative to the efficient outcome. Another interesting feature of these models is that in adverse-selection models the distance between the demand curve and the marginal-cost curve is the risk premium; in advantageous-selection models this is not true — that distance is the risk premium net of any fixed costs. (References: Einav, Finkelstein, and Cullen (2010); Einav, Finkelstein (2011).)
